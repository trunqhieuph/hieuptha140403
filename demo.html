F-Camp

In September of autumn, new students from FPT University will be welcomed by a jubilant atmosphere of the festival welcoming F-Camp newcomer. This program is organized under the companionship of International Cooperation & Personal Development (IC-PDP), FPT University. F-Camp is currently an annual program, an invaluable gift that all students of the course are giving to new students, and is also a place where you are infused with the fire of communication. Message: live and dedicate yourself to your 4-year student life.

Not all university students who are new to the university have participated in many interesting and interesting activities, and it is not easy to have many opportunities for them to shine and express themselves. With FPT University, on the contrary, F-Camp is one of the attractive activities for "rookies" because of the thoughtful and creative preparation of the senior students who are studying, which is the ideal place. thought for you to freely struggle and create, sometimes the new students are "NOT ENOUGHT" to enjoy all the activities and atmosphere of the festival!
